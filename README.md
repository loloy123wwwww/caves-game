# The cave game

---

### Objective:

- find a way out of cave while fighting enemies, jumping over lava, spikes and collecting coins
- get all coins in a level to continue your journey  
- click E near purple mushrooms to regenerate some HP  
- we painted custom assets packs to fit the game.

---

### Gameplay:

You start as a lost knight in a cave.  
Then you slowly get closer to the surface

You have to attack enemies, jump over lava and spikes and much more
